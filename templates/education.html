What is Dengue?

Dengue is a mosquito-borne viral infection that causes flu-like illness and can develop into severe complications.

  • High Fever
  • Severe Headache
  • Muscle Pain
  • Skin Rash
Dengue Prevention Guide Warning Signs & Symptoms Community Prevention Tips

Prevention Tips

  • Remove Standing Water — Empty containers, flower pots, and gutters regularly.
  • Use Protection — Wear long sleeves and use mosquito repellent.
  • Peak Hours — Avoid outdoor activities during dawn and dusk.
